JETS CLUB

Junior Engineers, Technicians, and Scientists (JETS) Club is a science club for students that aims to develop their scientific and technical skills. The club's activities include projects, competitions, and excursions.

What we do

  • Help students understand science and technology
  • Help students develop practical skills
  • Help students learn how to apply scientific theory to solve problems
  • Help students learn how to design and carry out projects
